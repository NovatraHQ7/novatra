# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We want Novatra to be a respectful, constructive space for everyone.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and professional in language and tone.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Offer actionable feedback focused on code, decisions, and outcomes.
- Welcome contributors from different backgrounds and skill levels.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Sexualized language or unwanted advances.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of discussions, reviews, or community spaces.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, code reviews, discussions, and chat channels related to this repository.

## Reporting

To report a violation, email: maintainers@novatrahq.com

Please include:

- What happened
- Where it happened
- Links/screenshots if available
- Any immediate safety concerns

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take action they consider appropriate, including warning, temporary suspension, or permanent ban from project spaces.

We will review reports promptly and keep reporter details as private as possible.
